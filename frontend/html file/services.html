ILAYA HEALTH CENTER SERVICES

We offer quality and accessible healthcare services for every resident of Barangay Ilaya.

Immunization

Providing free vaccines to infants, children, and adults to protect against preventable diseases and promote community immunity.

Issuance of Medical Certificate

Certified medical assessments and documentation needed for employment, school, or legal purposes.

Pre Natal / Post Natal

Comprehensive care and regular checkups for mothers before and after childbirth to ensure the health of both mother and baby.

Diagnostic

Basic diagnostic services and laboratory tests to help detect and monitor medical conditions early and accurately.

Outpatient Consultation

Free or low-cost consultations with licensed physicians for common illnesses and minor medical concerns.

Signing Death Certificate / Free Burial or Cremation

Official processing and certification of death documents, as well as assistance in availing city-sponsored burial or cremation services.

Availing Greencard / Hospitalization Services

Assisting residents in applying for the City Health Green Card program and coordinating access to partner hospitals for treatment.

Dental Care

Providing free dental checkups, tooth extractions, and oral hygiene education to maintain healthy smiles in the community.

Family Planning Services

Offering counseling and access to modern family planning methods to promote responsible parenthood and maternal health.
